The Renaissance was a period of time in Europe that lasted from the 14th to the 17th century. It was a time of great cultural and intellectual change, and it marked the transition from the Middle Ages to modern times.
Q: What were some of the key characteristics of the Renaissance?
A: The Renaissance was characterized by a renewed interest in classical learning, a focus on humanism, and a new emphasis on individualism and secularism. It was also a time of great artistic and scientific achievement.
